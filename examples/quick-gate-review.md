# Example: Quick Gate Review

Machine-checkable result: `quick-gate-review.json`.

## Product Direction

Change the empty-state copy on a setup page to reduce confusion.

## Mode

Quick Gate.

## Verdict

`continue`.

## Why

The change is reversible, low risk, and tied to a known confusion point.

## UX3 Review

| Flow | Result |
|---|---|
| User Flow | Users reach the empty state after connecting no sources and do not know what to do next. |
| Evidence Flow | Support notes and session replays show repeated hesitation at the same page. |
| Business Flow | Clearer setup may improve activation without changing product scope. |

## Evidence And Confidence

| Field | Answer |
|---|---|
| Evidence tier | t1 |
| Confidence level | Medium |
| Riskiest claim | Clearer copy will improve first-action completion. |
| Main uncertainty | Whether copy or missing guidance causes the hesitation. |

## Constraints

| Field | Answer |
|---|---|
| Scope | Edit empty-state headline and action label. |
| Non-goals | Do not redesign setup, add new automation, or change onboarding flow. |
| Measurement | Track click-through to first setup action. |
| Stop condition | Stop if users still hesitate or support questions do not change. |

## Context Pack

Execution may change copy and one action label.

Execution must not change layout, permissions, data handling, or onboarding steps.
