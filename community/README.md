# Community Evidence Loop

UX3 grows through field evidence, not trend accumulation.

## Lifecycle

1. A Contributor submits Field evidence through the Issue form.
2. An Evidence Steward checks consent, redaction, source quality,
   counter-signals, and limitations.
3. Maintainers classify the report as evidence, counterexample, pressure case,
   decision-rule candidate, or documentation input.
4. A Pull Request turns the accepted learning into a bounded repository change.
5. Tests and a second review protect the canonical contract.
6. Later outcomes may confirm, narrow, reverse, or retire the change.

## Strategic Scope

Visual quality, prototypes, and animation matter when they change product
judgment. The community focus is the strategic layer that precedes and guides
those artifacts:

- who the product is for and who bears its effects;
- what evidence is strong enough for the next action;
- how value, cost, risk, and durability move between stakeholders;
- what an agent may execute; and
- what must remain Designer-in-the-Loop.

Start with `CONTRIBUTING.md`. Governance and decision rights are defined in
`GOVERNANCE.md`.
